Assignment

For this week's project work, we exchanged feedback on project drafts during lab. We came to lab and worked with our group to share Project Draft 1 for peer feedback.

For this assignment, we submitted a copy of the written feedback we gave to another group, including which group we reviewed and the feedback itself.

First Group: AB8 (Xintong Hou, Daniel Yan)

Project: Rate Our Courses - Website

Feedback I gave:

  • The website has an effective design, and the concept of rating courses instead of professors is a strong, valuable idea.
  • The overall color palette could be more consistent across the site. The purple, while fitting the UW theme, stands out significantly and could be better integrated.
  • The mobile version is not fully responsive on inspection. Implementing a media query is necessary to ensure the display adapts correctly to smaller screens.

Second Group: AB3 (Sharesse, Maya, An)

Project: Husky Circulo - Website

Feedback I gave:

  • The website has a very clear goal and it fixes what most UW Students face when either moving out or moving in with their furniture.
  • I like how responsive it's responsive in mobile and how much it resembles Facebook Marketplace.
  • Maybe for the navbar, rather than making it at the top, they could make it on the side like how Facebook does it.
