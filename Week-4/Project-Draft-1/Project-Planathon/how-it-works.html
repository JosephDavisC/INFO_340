How It Works

1. Create Your Hackathon

Set up your event with a name, date, and basic info. No more juggling between Google Sheets, Docs, and Discord - everything lives in one place.

2. Build Your Organizing Team

Create teams for different areas like logistics, marketing, sponsorship, and judging. Invite organizers and volunteers, then assign them to the right teams.

3. Create and Assign Tasks

Break down the work into tasks. Assign them to team members, set priority levels (high, medium, low), and track progress as things get done. Filter and reorganize tasks as your planning evolves.

4. Track Your Milestones

Add important deadlines to your dashboard - registration opening, sponsor confirmation due dates, schedule releases, and more. See what's coming up and what's already been completed.
