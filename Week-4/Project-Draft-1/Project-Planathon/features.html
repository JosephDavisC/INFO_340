Features

Planathon helps hackathon organizers manage everything in one place, from milestones and internal tasks to team responsibilities and planning details.

Milestone Dashboard

Track key dates like registration deadlines, sponsor confirmations, and schedule releases so your team always knows what’s next.

  • Add milestones with due dates
  • See what’s coming up at a glance
  • Reduce last-minute scrambling

Task Management

Create tasks, assign them to team members, set priorities, and mark them complete - without needing a separate spreadsheet.

  • Create + assign tasks
  • Set priority (low / medium / high)
  • Mark complete + track progress

Team Management

Organize your crew by team (logistics, marketing, sponsorship, judging) and make responsibilities clear.

  1. Create organizing teams
  2. Assign members + roles
  3. Know who owns what

Planning Hub

Store key planning info like sponsor details, prizes, workshop schedules, and judging criteria so it’s not scattered across random docs.

  • Sponsor contacts + notes
  • Prize list + requirements
  • Workshops + judging criteria
