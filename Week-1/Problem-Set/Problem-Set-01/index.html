Mark Watney

Mark Watney is an astronaut who gets stranded on Mars and has to survive on his own. He is the main character in The Martian, where he uses science, engineering, and problem solving to stay alive.

Why I admire him

  • He stays calm and focuses on solving one problem at a time.
  • He relies on logic, data, and experimentation instead of guessing.
  • He keeps a positive attitude even when things go very wrong.
